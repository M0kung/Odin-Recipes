Cook this wonderful vegan Risotto with confidence!

This creamy vegan risotto is rich in flavor and perfect for a comforting meal. Made with arborio rice and fresh vegetables, it's a delightful dish that can be customized to your liking.

This is what is inside:

  • 1 cup arborio rice
  • onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup peas (fresh or frozen)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    •

    This is how it is done:

    1. Sauté Aromatics: In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, sautéing until the onion is translucent.
    2. Toast the Rice: Stir in the arborio rice, cooking for about 2-3 minutes until the rice is lightly toasted.
    3. Add Broth Gradually: Slowly add the vegetable broth, one ladle at a time, stirring frequently until the liquid is absorbed before adding more. Continue until the rice is creamy and al dente (about 20 minutes).
    4. Stir in Veggies: In the last 5 minutes of cooking, add the sliced mushrooms and peas, stirring until they are heated through. Serve warm and enjoy!
