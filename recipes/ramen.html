This vibrant vegan ramen is packed with flavor and nutrients, making it a comforting bowl of goodness. With a rich broth and an array of fresh toppings, itâ€™s perfect for any time of year.

This is what is inside:

  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 packs of ramen noodles (discard the seasoning packets)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 cup bok choy, chopped
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • Green onions, for garnish
    •

    This is how it is done:

    1. Prepare the Broth: In a large pot, bring the vegetable broth to a simmer. Add soy sauce and stir well.
    2. Cook the Noodles: Add the ramen noodles to the simmering broth and cook according to package instructions, usually about 3-4 minutes.
    3. Add Vegetables: In the last 2 minutes of cooking, add the bok choy, carrot, and mushrooms, stirring until the vegetables are tender.
    4. Serve and Garnish: Ladle the ramen into bowls and garnish with sliced green onions. Enjoy your delicious vegan ramen!
